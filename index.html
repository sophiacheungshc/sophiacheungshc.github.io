A bit about me

I am a software engineer specialized in frontend and full-stack web development.

I was born in Hong Kong and raised in San Diego/Los Angeles for the latter half of my teenagehood. I had received my Bachelor's degree at University of California, San Diego, and spent most of my early adulthood exploring the healthcare field, from animal to human medicine. In my free time, I like to indulge in snowboarding, thrift shopping, jamming to EDM, and lots of good gelato.

-

Today I am a developer. My passion for computers had stemmed from fond childhood memories of playing and 'hacking' Miniclip web games and MMORPGs with my cousin, and from furthering this interest by taking an introductory course to Java in college, I decided to study coding full time. Now I make games that I once played. I have found the perfect outlet for my creative energy in frontend web development.

My Skills

I code in Javascript, Ruby, and with a multitude of frameworks such as React/Redux, Ruby on Rails, Node.js, and Express.js.
